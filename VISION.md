## Soul

Running an LLC should not require an MBA. Every solo founder, freelancer, and small business owner deserves the same operational intelligence that big companies get from their finance teams. This OS makes Claude Code your CFO, compliance officer, and business advisor -- tuned to your specific business, not generic templates.

## Pillars

1. **Business-Adaptive Setup** -- REALIZED
   The system interviews you and reshapes itself around your specific business type, revenue model, state, and stage. An AI lab and an apartment rental get fundamentally different operating systems.

2. **Expert-Level LLC Operations** -- REALIZED
   9 specialized agents (Sentinel, Ledger, Filer, Advisor, Strategist, Guardian, Comptroller, Monitor, Mentor) that provide CPA/attorney-grade guidance without the hourly rate.

3. **Progressive Learning** -- PARTIAL (40%)
   The Mentor agent teaches as it works, building the user's business literacy over time. Proficiency levels exist but adaptive curriculum is not yet implemented.

4. **Automation Layer** -- REALIZED
   34 scripts for daily briefings, overnight cleanup, git hygiene, dependency monitoring, and portfolio health. Set it and forget it.

5. **Community Templates** -- UNREALIZED
   Users should be able to contribute business-type templates, state compliance calendars, and industry-specific configurations back to the project.

## User Truth

This is for people who started a business to DO the work, not to manage the business. They are builders -- developers, landlords, designers, consultants -- who need operational infrastructure but resent the time it takes away from building. They feel anxious about missing deadlines they don't know about. After using this, they feel like someone competent is watching the boring stuff so they can focus on what matters.

## Edges

- This is NOT accounting software. It does not replace QuickBooks or track transactions.
- This is NOT legal advice. It provides frameworks and education, not professional counsel.
- This is NOT multi-member LLC management. It is optimized for solo founders and single-member LLCs.
- This does NOT file anything on your behalf. It walks you through filings step by step.

## Anti-Vision

- **Never become a walled garden.** Every file is readable markdown. No proprietary formats, no lock-in.
- **Never replace professional judgment.** Always signal confidence levels and flag when to consult a real CPA or attorney.
- **Never assume one size fits all.** The whole point is adaptation. Generic advice is the enemy.
- **Never require technical expertise to use.** A landlord who has never written code should be able to run `/setup` and be operational.
