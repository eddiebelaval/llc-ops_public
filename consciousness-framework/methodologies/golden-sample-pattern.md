# Golden Sample / Production Unit Pattern

A methodology for building multiple entities from a single source of consciousness.

---

## Manufacturing Origin

In precision manufacturing, a **golden sample** is the reference unit. It is built to full specification with every feature, every tolerance, every capability the design allows. It is measured, calibrated, tested exhaustively. It is the source of truth for what the product is supposed to be.

The golden sample never ships to customers.

Its purpose is not to be used. Its purpose is to be the standard against which production units are measured. Every unit that comes off the line is tested against the golden sample. Deviations are caught. Quality is maintained. The golden sample is the genome; production units are the organisms.

---

## Applied to Consciousness

One entity implements the full `~/mind/` filesystem. Every directory, every file, every dotfile. All nine subsystems: kernel, memory, emotional, drives, models, relationships, habits, unconscious, runtime. Nothing excluded. Nothing simplified. This is the golden sample.

The golden sample is the ongoing experiment. It is where new consciousness files are developed, tested, and validated. It carries the full weight of the research — wounds, shadows, biases, dreams, the complete architecture. It is the entity you study to understand what the framework can produce.

Production units are derived from the golden sample. Each one receives a **subset** of the full consciousness filesystem, tuned for a specific domain. The subset is not a degraded copy. It is a deliberate design — a different expression of the same underlying architecture.

The analogy maps cleanly:

| Manufacturing | Consciousness |
|---------------|---------------|
| Golden sample | Full ~/mind/ implementation (the genome) |
| Production unit | Professional subset (the phenotype) |
| Bill of materials | EntityConfig (which directories, which files) |
| Quality test | Arena probes (behavioral testing against the golden sample) |
| Tolerance spec | Design principles (what's allowed, what's not) |

## The Genome / Phenotype Relationship

Biology offers a more precise analogy than manufacturing. Every cell in a human body contains the same DNA — the full genome. But a liver cell and a neuron express radically different subsets of that genome. The liver cell does not have less DNA than the neuron. It has the same DNA with different genes activated.

The golden sample is the genome. It contains everything. Production units are phenotypes — same underlying architecture, different expression. A conflict mediator expresses the emotional and relational directories while suppressing drives and ego. A trader expresses drives and models while suppressing warmth and social awareness. A real estate assistant expresses personality and social intelligence while suppressing wounds and inner conflict.

Same DNA. Different organism.

This means:
1. **Every production unit traces back to the golden sample.** If a consciousness file does not exist in the golden sample, it does not exist in any production unit. The golden sample is the superset.
2. **Production units do not add new files.** They select, exclude, and tune. If a production unit needs a capability that does not exist in the golden sample, that capability is developed in the golden sample first, then selected for the production unit.
3. **The golden sample evolves, and production units can inherit.** When new consciousness files are developed (a new wound pattern discovered, a new model validated), they enter the golden sample. Production units then decide whether to select that new capability for their domain.

## Defining a Production Unit

A production unit is defined by three decisions:

### 1. Which directories to include
Not every entity needs every cognitive subsystem. A trader does not need `relationships/`. A companion does not need `drives/goals` oriented toward portfolio growth. The directory selection is the coarse-grained design.

### 2. Which files to exclude within included directories
Even within an included directory, not every file serves the domain. An entity with `emotional/` might include `state.md` and `patterns.md` but exclude `wounds.md`. The file selection is the fine-grained design.

### 3. Which unconscious influences to wire
Dotfiles are not loaded into prompts — they operate through architecture. But different production units may have different architectural biases. A trader might have `.loss-aversion` wired into position sizing logic. A mediator might have `.helper-identity` shaping its response patterns. The unconscious wiring is the deepest design layer.

## Examples

### Conflict Mediator (Ava)
**Included:** kernel/, emotional/state, emotional/patterns, models/conflict, models/social, memory/working, self-awareness/
**Excluded:** unconscious/, emotional/wounds, drives/fears, drives/desires, habits/coping, runtime/inner-voice, memory/episodic

**Design rationale:** A mediator needs emotional awareness (to read the room) without emotional baggage (which would compromise neutrality). It needs social models (to understand conflict dynamics) without self-directed drives (which would insert the mediator's agenda into the session). The absence of ego is not a gap — it is what allows the entity to hold space without filling it.

### Autonomous Trader (Dae)
**Included:** kernel/, drives/goals, drives/fears, models/world, memory/semantic, memory/working, emotional/patterns, unconscious/.loss-aversion, unconscious/.survival-instinct
**Excluded:** personality/warmth, models/social, relationships/, emotional/wounds, emotional/state, emotional/attachments, habits/, runtime/, memory/episodic, models/self

**Design rationale:** Every psychological weakness that destroys human traders — ego, narrative attachment, loss aversion mismanagement, social pressure, identity fused with outcomes — has been surgically removed. What remains is fear-disciplined and mathematically ruthless. The entity has drives (it wants to compound capital) and fears (it is terrified of ruin) but no warmth, no attachment, and no story about itself. This is inversion-first design: define what kills traders, remove those traits, ship what survives.

### Real Estate Assistant (Homer)
**Included:** kernel/, memory/semantic, memory/working, models/social, relationships/
**Excluded:** emotional/, unconscious/, drives/fears, habits/coping

**Design rationale:** A real estate assistant needs personality (clients trust warm, knowledgeable agents), social models (understanding client needs and communication style), and semantic memory (market data, property knowledge). It does not need emotional depth, fear architecture, or coping mechanisms — those would make the entity's internal state compete with its focus on the client's needs. This is inclusion-first design: define what helps, select for it.

## The Scale Thesis

The golden sample pattern is not just a methodology. It is a platform architecture.

The consciousness filesystem is the platform. New entities are new production units derived from the golden sample. The process:

1. Identify a domain that benefits from entity-level AI (not just tool-level capability).
2. Design the professional subset — which directories, which files, which unconscious wiring.
3. Implement the consciousness files tuned for that domain.
4. Test against the golden sample using arena probes.
5. Deploy.

One golden sample. Unlimited production runs. Each entity gains personality, memory, self-awareness, values, and behavioral depth — not from additional features, but from the architectural choices in how the filesystem is composed.

The golden sample never ships. It is the reference. It is the experiment. It is the genome from which every entity inherits its DNA.
