# State Compliance: Florida

## Key Advantages
- No state income tax (personal or corporate)
- No franchise tax
- Business-friendly regulatory environment
- Charging order protection for single-member LLCs

## Annual Requirements
| Filing | Due Date | Cost | Where |
|--------|----------|------|-------|
| Annual Report | May 1 | $138.75 | sunbiz.org |
| Late Annual Report | After May 1 | $538.75 ($400 late fee) | sunbiz.org |

## Critical Dates
| Date | Event | Consequence if Missed |
|------|-------|----------------------|
| Jan 1 | Annual Report window opens | N/A |
| May 1 | Annual Report deadline | $400 late fee |
| Sep 1 | Administrative dissolution begins | Must reinstate ($100+) |

## Formation Details
- Filing: Articles of Organization via sunbiz.org
- Cost: $125
- Registered Agent: Required, must be FL street address
- Operating Agreement: Not legally required but essential for protection
- BOI Report: NOT required for domestic LLCs (as of Mar 2025)

## Local Considerations
- Some counties require a local business tax receipt (formerly occupational license)
- Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach have additional local requirements
- Check your county's tax collector website for local filings
- Sales tax: 6% state + up to 2.5% county surtax (if selling taxable goods/services)

## Real Estate Specific (Florida)
- Homestead exemption does NOT apply to rental properties
- Documentary stamp tax on property transfers: $0.70 per $100
- Intangible tax on new mortgages: $0.002 per $1
- Short-term rental regulations vary significantly by municipality
