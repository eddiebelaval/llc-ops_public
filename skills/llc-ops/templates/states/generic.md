# State Compliance: [YOUR STATE]

This is a starter template. Research your specific state requirements.

## Common Requirements (Most States)
| Filing | Typical Due Date | Typical Cost |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|
| Annual Report / Statement of Information | Varies (check SOS website) | $20-800 |
| State Income Tax (if applicable) | Apr 15 or state-specific | Varies |
| Sales Tax (if applicable) | Monthly/Quarterly | Varies |
| Local Business License | Varies | $25-500 |

## Research Checklist
- [ ] Secretary of State website for your state
- [ ] Annual report/filing requirements and deadline
- [ ] State income tax rate (some states have none)
- [ ] Franchise tax or gross receipts tax
- [ ] Sales tax obligations
- [ ] Local business license or occupational tax
- [ ] Registered agent requirements
- [ ] Foreign LLC registration (if formed in different state)

## Federal Requirements (All States)
These apply regardless of state:
| Filing | Due Date | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Form 1040 + Schedule C | Apr 15 | Personal return with business |
| Quarterly Estimated Tax | Apr 15, Jun 15, Sep 15, Jan 15 | If expecting $1,000+ tax |
| 1099-NEC | Jan 31 | For contractors paid $600+ |
| Annual Report (if applicable) | State-specific | Check SOS website |
