# LLC-Ops: Tech / AI Business Profile

## Expense Categories

### 100% Deductible
| Category | Examples | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Software & SaaS | Claude Pro, OpenAI API, GitHub, Supabase, Vercel, AWS | Monthly subscriptions = current expense |
| Cloud & Hosting | AWS, GCP, Azure, DigitalOcean, domains | Infrastructure costs, scale with usage |
| API Costs | LLM tokens, third-party APIs, data services | Track per-project for profitability |
| Hardware (under $2,500) | Keyboards, monitors, cables, peripherals | De minimis safe harbor |
| Professional Services | CPA, attorney, contractors, designers | 1099 required if $600+ |
| Education & Research | Courses, papers, books, conferences | Must relate to current business |
| Marketing & Growth | Ads, content tools, analytics, SEO | Track ROI per channel |
| Communication | Phone, internet (business %) | Document business portion |

### R&D Tax Credit Eligible
Your AI/software work likely qualifies for the R&D tax credit:
- Developing new algorithms or models
- Building novel software functionality
- Improving existing systems with technical uncertainty
- Experimentation with new architectures

**Documentation required:** Project descriptions, time logs, technical challenges encountered.
Up to $250,000 can offset payroll taxes for qualifying startups.

## Tax Strategy Priorities
1. **R&D Tax Credit** -- Most underused credit for software companies
2. **Section 179** -- Expense equipment in year 1 (GPU purchases, etc.)
3. **QBI Deduction** -- 20% deduction if under income threshold
4. **SEP IRA / Solo 401(k)** -- Tax-deferred retirement contributions
5. **S-Corp Election** -- When profit exceeds ~$50k after reasonable salary
6. **Home Office** -- Dedicated workspace deduction

## Insurance Recommendations
| Type | Priority | Estimated Cost | Why |
|------|----------|---------------|-----|
| Cyber Liability | HIGH | $500-1,500/yr | Data handling, API keys, client data |
| Professional Liability (E&O) | HIGH | $500-1,200/yr | Errors in software/AI output |
| General Liability | MEDIUM | $400-800/yr | General business protection |

## Key Risk Areas
- **IP Ownership:** Ensure contractor agreements assign IP to your LLC
- **AI Liability:** Document model limitations, add disclaimers for AI output
- **Data Privacy:** CCPA/GDPR compliance if handling user data
- **Contractor vs Employee:** Misclassification risk with regular contractors
- **Open Source Licensing:** Track licenses in dependencies
