## Tax Strategy Priorities
1. **Depreciation** -- 27.5-year schedule reduces taxable income significantly
2. **Cost Segregation Study** -- Accelerate depreciation on building components (worth it for properties $500k+)
3. **1031 Exchange** -- Defer capital gains by rolling into new property
4. **QBI Deduction** -- 20% if rental qualifies as business (safe harbor: 250+ hrs/yr)
5. **Passive Activity Rules** -- Real estate professional status unlocks unlimited loss deductions
6. **Repairs vs Improvements** -- Repairs are immediate deductions; improvements must be depreciated

### Real Estate Professional Status
If you spend 750+ hours AND more time in real estate than any other activity:
- Rental losses can offset W-2 or other active income
- No $25,000 passive loss limitation
- **Documentation is critical:** Keep detailed time logs

### 1031 Exchange Rules
- Must identify replacement property within 45 days
- Must close within 180 days
- Must use a Qualified Intermediary (cannot touch the funds)
- Like-kind: any real property for any real property
- Boot (cash received) is taxable

## Insurance Recommendations
| Type | Priority | Estimated Cost | Why |
|------|----------|---------------|-----|
| Landlord Policy | REQUIRED | $1,000-2,500/yr per property | Property damage, liability |
| Umbrella Liability | HIGH | $200-500/yr per $1M | Extends liability beyond base policy |
| Flood Insurance | VARIES | $500-3,000/yr | Required in flood zones, wise elsewhere |
| STR Rider/Policy | HIGH (if STR) | $1,500-3,000/yr | Standard landlord policies exclude STR |
| Loss of Rent | MEDIUM | Included or $100-300/yr | Covers income during uninhabitable periods |

## Key Risk Areas
- **Tenant Liability:** Slip-and-fall, injury on property. Umbrella policy essential.
- **Fair Housing:** Never discriminate. Document all tenant screening criteria.
- **Local STR Regulations:** Many cities require licenses, cap nights, or ban STR entirely.
- **Security Deposits:** State laws vary on amount, holding, and return timeline.
- **Lead Paint / Asbestos:** Disclosure required for pre-1978 properties.
- **Commingling:** Never use rental income for personal expenses without proper distribution.

## Tracking Recommendations
Track per-property:
- Monthly rental income
- Occupancy rate (STR)
- Maintenance costs
- Net operating income (NOI)
- Cash-on-cash return
- Capital expenditure reserve (budget 5-10% of rent)
